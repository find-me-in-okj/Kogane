KOGANE -- FEATURE GUIDE
WATCHLIST
Your watchlist is the set of patterns Kogane checks every incoming certificate against. Patterns are saved to localStorage and persist across sessions. keyword -- Matches if the term appears in, or fuzzy-matches a segment of, any domain. Best for brand names, product names, internal codenames. Example: "paypal" catches paypal.com, secure-paypal.net, paypa1.io domain -- Checks full domains for exact clone, TLD swap, homoglyph, subdomain abuse, typosquat. Best for specific domains you own or monitor. Example: "google.com" catches g00gle.com, google.net, login.google.com.phish.io Adding a new pattern immediately re-scores the existing feed. Any recent cert matching the new pattern is retroactively flagged. Click [x] on any entry to remove it.
CERTIFICATE FEED (center)
Live stream of incoming TLS certificates, newest at top. Normal certs -- dimmed text, no flag Hit certs -- bold text, [!] flag, match label + score pills, green background Click any hit row to open the detail inspector at the bottom. ALL -- show every cert in the stream HITS ONLY -- show only certs matching your watchlist Search box -- live filter by domain text
DETAIL INSPECTOR (bottom strip)
Click any hit in the feed or alerts panel to inspect it. Shows: issuer, detection time, all SANs in the cert, match count. Per-match breakdown: matched domain vs watched pattern, detection method, score.
ACTIVE ALERTS (right panel)
Chronological log of every hit this session, most recent first. Click any alert row to open it in the detail inspector. Selected alert is highlighted with a left border.
HIT NOTIFICATIONS
Every hit triggers a notification in the bottom-right corner showing the Kogane buddy, "DING-DONG DING-DONG DING-DONG", the matched domain, threat level, and score. Notifications auto-dismiss after 6 seconds. Click [X] to dismiss early. Multiple hits queue and display sequentially.
MATCHING ENGINE -- FIVE LAYERS
Exact clone Score 100 -- domain registered as exact match (cross-TLD shadow) Homoglyph Score 91 -- paypa1.com, rnicosoft.com Subdomain abuse Score 88 -- login.paypal.com.attacker.net TLD swap Score 82 -- paypal.net, google.io Typosquat Score 72-86 -- Levenshtein distance <= 2 Brand embedded Score 72 -- paypal-secure-login.com Keyword present Score 72 -- keyword anywhere in domain Fuzzy keyword Score 43-68 -- fuzzy per-segment match Threat levels: CRITICAL 90+ | HIGH 75+ | MEDIUM 55+ | LOW <55
